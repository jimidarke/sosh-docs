# Setting up staff phones

Your store handhelds arrive with the Sovereign Shelf app already installed and locked to the job — there's no app store, nothing to install, and nothing for staff to misconfigure. This module is the owner's side of the phones: introducing each one to your store, and deciding which features your staff see.

**Before you start:** finish the [Getting Started track](../../getting-started/index.md) first. [Train your team](../../getting-started/a7-train-your-team.md) already walked you through pairing your first phone the short way — the lessons here go deeper.

## The lessons

1. [Pair a phone](d2-pair-a-phone.md) — the one-time introduction between a phone and your Commander, screen by screen.
2. [Choose which features staff see](d5-feature-toggles.md) — five switches that trim the app down to the workflows your store actually uses.

More phone lessons are coming soon: the two PINs (staff and manager), the staff roster in depth, and caring for the phone fleet — batteries, replacements, and moving a phone between stores.

## For the people holding the phones

The staff-facing side lives in its own track — short, task-only lessons your team can read on the floor. Start them with [Look up a product](../../staff/f2-look-up-a-product.md) and [Update a shelf tag](../../staff/f3-update-a-shelf-tag.md).
