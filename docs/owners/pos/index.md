# Connecting your POS

Every price on your shelves comes from your point-of-sale system (POS). This module connects your Commander to your POS — whichever one you run — and shows you how to keep the two in step. Once the connection is made, there is nothing to maintain: change a price at the register, and the shelf follows on its own.

**Before you start:** finish the [Getting Started track](../../getting-started/index.md) first. In particular, [Connect your product data](../../getting-started/a4-connect-your-product-data.md) gives you the five-minute orientation this module builds on.

One thing to know up front: your Commander talks to exactly **one** POS source at a time. Saving a connection on one tab of the POS page replaces whatever was saved before — so pick your system's lesson below and stick with it.
